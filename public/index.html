      Understanding Market Volatility

      What is Volatility?
      Market volatility reflects how much a coin’s price fluctuates over a given period. High volatility indicates sharp price swings, while low volatility suggests more stable, consistent pricing.

      How the Score Works:
      The volatility score (out of 100) is based on recent 24h and 7d price movements across top cryptocurrencies.

      🔥 💥 🌋 🚨 70–100: High volatility (riskier, potential for big gains or losses)
      🌪️ 🌊 ⛅ 🌀 40–69: Moderate volatility (some movement)
      🧘 😴 🛌 🪨 0–39: Low volatility (stable, safer)
